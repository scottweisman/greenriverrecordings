An Exceptional Recording Studio

Green River Recordings is a state of the art recording studio that regularly records bands of all musical styles and spots for radio, TV and the internet. The studio features a 16x17 control room, a spacious 31x21.5 tracking room, three isolation rooms including our vocal booth. The entire facility is "floated" for near perfect sound isolation and treated with custom built acoustics for versatile control of room ambiance.
