The Experience:

It is easy to make Green River feel like home. Today it’s your studio; come in, sit on the couch, put your feet on the coffee table and just make great music. Get comfortable, get inspired, and let Ben and the guys do the rest. When you rent out Green River Recordings you get the place, a highly trained engineer, and all the toys. Several drums kits, percussion instruments, keyboards, amps and guitars come with the space rental so you can find your sound. With so many sounds within arms length, Green River makes it easy to create and experiment.

The Studio:

Green River is a 3,000 square foot recording studio in Maple Grove (Hemlock and 94W). It has 3 isolation booths, large tracking and control rooms, and plenty of space to spread out while tracking a full band. Unlike many studios in town, Green River was built as a recording studio. It has floated floors that help with the isolation and management of sound. The acoustics are custom designed, the entry to the building and to the studio require swipe cards for access, and there’s enough free parking for everyone.

Previous Work:

Green River Recordings has worked in nearly every genre of music from metal to bluegrass, rap to singer/songwriter, the staff at Green River love chasing the sounds you hear in your head and then capturing the performance in Pro Tools.We’ve worked with some of the best; Dick Dale, Zach “Reazon” Heiligman (Foster the People, DAE), Shaun Labelle (Ray Charles, Jody Watley), Gin Strings, Space Monster, Voces del Rancho, Ashley DuBose (The Voice), Daryl Boudreaux (Sound of Blackness), Bollywood, Lisa Keith Bernard (Janet Jackson etc.), Eric Gordon (American Idol), Endia Howze, Exclusive Class and Fanaka just to name a few.
